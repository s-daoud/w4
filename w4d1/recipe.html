Cottage Pie

A recipe for making cottage pie!

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients!

For the meat:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 celery stalks
  • Peas
  • Corn
  • 4-5 cloves garlic
  • Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Red wine
  • 1 can beef broth
  • Worchestershire sauce
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For the potatoes:

  • 3-4 potatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheddar cheese

Description!

Cottage pie is a delicious meat pie with a mashed potato topping. It is a variation of shepherd's pie, but where shepherd's pie uses lamb or mutton (thus shepherd!), cottage pie is made with beef instead. Meat is cooked down with vegetables into a gravy and then mashed potatoes are placed on top.

Directions!

  1. Brown the ground beef and set aside.
  2. Dice the onion, celery, and carrots. Sweat in a pan until everything is soft and onions are translucent, approximately 20 minutes.
  3. Add peas and corn and let them cook through. Turn up heat, add garlic.
  4. Add a few squeezes of a sugar-free ketchup and the flour to thicken the gravy. Fry it all up.
  5. Add the meat back to the pan. Deglaze with wine and cook down.
  6. Add the beef broth, a few dashes of worchestershire sauce, 3-4 pinches of rosemary, many dashes of thyme, and a bay leaf. Salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Simmer for 45 minutes. Add more flour to thicken if needed.
  8. Meanwhile, boil the potatoes and garlic.
  9. Drain and mash the potatoes. Add butter, cream, salt, and pepper, to desired consistency and taste.
  10. Put meat in a baking dish. Top with potatoes and grated cheese.
  11. Bake for 25 minutes at 400 degrees.
  12. Let cool and enjoy!
